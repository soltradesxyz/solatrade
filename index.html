FAQ

What wallets are supported?
We support Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, and any wallet compatible with the Solana Wallet Standard.
What is the mint price?
The genesis collection mints at 0.85 SOL per NFT. A small Solana network fee applies on top.
Is there a limit per wallet?
Each wallet can mint up to 5 NFTs during the public phase. Whitelist members get early access with a 3-mint limit.
Where can I trade after minting?
All SolTrades NFTs are listed on Magic Eden and Tensor automatically after mint. You can also list on any Solana-compatible marketplace.
Are there royalties?
Yes, a 5% creator royalty is enforced on-chain via Metaplex. This funds ongoing development and community rewards.
